Help and FAQs

This site hopes to collect amazing list of courses (videos, blogs or anything) found online, backed up by learners who found those courses amazing.

There are only two ways you could use this site.

  1. You are looking to learn something new.
    Enter your query in search box. Then, find out amazing resources you want to learn from.
  2. You want to share your opinion on a course.
    Share your opinion on already existing course. If the course does not exist, post the course along with your opinion.

Only editing of your opinion for course is allowed. Once you post the course, editing will be handled by community. This feature hasn't been implemented yet. So, double check before posting a course.

I plan to implement following features as implemented in other community sites.

  1. Reward for posting course, expressing your opinion, helping community grow (similar to karma in reddit, points and badges in stack overflow)
  2. Implementing upvotes.
  3. Implementing followers system, where you can follow post, tag or people.

Other things that can enrich this site can be:

  1. Users will be able to create a specialization, where they can combine multiple courses and create a curriculum.
  2. Using Web Scraping and Natural Language Processing, we could extract out information from the course webpage to enrich our search.
